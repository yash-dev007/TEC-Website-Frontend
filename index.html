About TEC

The Tech and Entrepreneurship Challenge is a premier competition designed to foster innovation and entrepreneurial spirit among students worldwide.

About TEC

TEC is more than just a competition – it's a launchpad for the next generation of entrepreneurs. Our space-themed challenge brings together brilliant minds from across the globe to solve real-world problems with innovative solutions. Participants will journey through four exciting phases, each designed to test different aspects of entrepreneurship – from ideation to execution, from pitching to networking. Join us in this cosmic adventure where your ideas can truly reach for the stars!

E-Cell MET

E-Cell MET is the official startup and innovation cell of MET Institute of Technology, focused on building entrepreneurial skills through workshops, ideathons, and competitions. Our achievements speak for our dedication — we secured AIR 1 at the National Entrepreneurship Challenge (NEC) by IIT Bombay and emerged as the 1st Runner-up at the Western Entrepreneurship Challenge (WEC). These milestones reflect our strong teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving approach. Supported by MET Institute of Technology, we thrive with institutional guidance, infrastructure, and a growing startup ecosystem on campus.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you on your entrepreneurial journey.

How do I register for TEC?

Registration is simple! Click on the Register button and fill out the form with your team details. Make sure to have your E-Cell registered before participating.

What is the team size limit?

Teams can have 3-5 members. Each team must represent a registered E-Cell from their respective institution.

Are there any participation fees?

No, participation in TEC is completely free. We believe in making entrepreneurship accessible to all students.

What are the prizes?

Winners will receive cash prizes, mentorship opportunities, incubation support, and networking opportunities with industry leaders.

Can international teams participate?

Yes! TEC welcomes participants from around the globe. Virtual participation options are available for international teams.

What kind of ideas are accepted?

We welcome innovative ideas across all sectors - technology, healthcare, education, sustainability, and more. The key is innovation and scalability.
